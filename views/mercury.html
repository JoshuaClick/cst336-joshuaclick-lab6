Welcome to Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to orbit the Sun, whizzing around it in a mere 88 days. It rotates around its poles in about 58 days--so a Mercury day takes up most of a Mercury year.

The surface is very moon-like in that it has lots of well preserved impact craters, indicating that there is very little weathering and geological activity.
