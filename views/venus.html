Welcome to Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun, and orbits around it every 224.7 arth days--or 1.92 Venus days.

Venus has the dubious distinction of having the densest atmosphere of all the terrestrial planets, with a surface pressure 92 times that of Earth's, as well as being the hottest, with a surface temperature of 735 degrees Kelvin.
